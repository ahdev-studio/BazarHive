Welcome to BazarHive

Your smart companion for managing shopping lists and personal inventory.

BazarHive helps you create, organize, and manage your shopping lists effortlessly. Never forget an item at the store again! You can also keep track of your household inventory.

For your peace of mind, BazarHive offers a secure backup feature using your own Google Drive. The app only accesses files created by itself within a dedicated 'BazarHive' folder and does not access any of your other personal files.
